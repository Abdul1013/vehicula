Our Services
Documents Renewal

With our installment payment program, you can save your money in little bits for your vehicle particulars renewal.

Read More Start chat

Most vehicle owners are required to register their vehicles with the Directorate of Road Traffic Service (VIO) upon initial purchase, and it is mandatory by law to renew the particulars annually.

We at Vehiculars exist to make you obtain these papers easily by providing you with our installment payment program. This way you can save your money in little bits for the particulars.

The following documents are renewable for both private and commercial vehicles in Nigeria (Abuja):

  • Vehicle License,
  • Road Worthiness,
  • Comprehensive Insurance,
  • Computerize inspection certificate,
  • Hackney Permit (if it is a commercial vehicle),
  • Heavy duty permit ( For trucks).

Local Government Permit/AMAC

Commercial vehicles (especially those with branding on them) are required to obtain the necessary permits and emblems to operate. Use Vehiculars to setup a convenient installment.

To operate commercial vehicles (often with company branding on them), its mandatory for the companies to obtain the necessary permits and emblems to operate. Most often, they also need to obtain advertisement permits. These documents are by local government offices nationwide.

Vehiculars will provide information on the necessary permits for your commercial vehicle category, and then offer you payment plans for you to keep money aside in installment using our technology platform.
